Welcome to Hazaribag College

Hazaribagh College of Dental Sciences & Hospital is situated in scenic locale of Hazaribagh city. The college was established in the year 2007 and is affiliated to the Vinoba Bhave University. It is the foremost dental college in Jharkhand was recognized by the Dental Council of India, New Delhi & Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India.
